# InterviewOps vs Real-Time Interview Assistants

This document explains how InterviewOps differs from tools that provide live interview
assistance.

---

## Summary

| Feature | InterviewOps | Real-time interview assistants |
|---------|:---:|:---:|
| Local-first (data stays on your machine) | ✅ | ❌ |
| Practice-only — no live interview assistance | ✅ | ❌ |
| Open-source core | ✅ | ❌ |
| Developer-specific tracks (Angular, React, AI) | ✅ | ❌ |
| Resume/JD-aware questions | ✅ | ✅ |
| Readiness reports & weakness maps | ✅ | ❌ |
| STAR story bank | ✅ | ❌ |
| Low cost — BYOK model | ✅ | ❌ |
| Ethics guardrails built in | ✅ | ❌ |
| Long-term skill building focus | ✅ | ❌ |

---

## Philosophy difference

Real-time interview assistants are designed to give you answers during a live interview
without the interviewer knowing. InterviewOps is designed to give you the skills and
confidence to answer those questions yourself.

We believe the second approach is:

- Better for your career
- Better for your mental health
- Better for the profession
- Aligned with most companies' interview policies

---

## What we build instead

- A practice environment that simulates real interviews
- Rubric-based feedback that tells you specifically what to improve
- Readiness reports that show your interview-readiness level
- STAR story banks that prepare your behavioral answers in advance
- Study plans that target your weakest areas
- Local-first architecture that keeps your data private
