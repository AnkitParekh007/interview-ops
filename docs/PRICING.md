# InterviewOps Pricing

InterviewOps follows an open-source-first, local-first pricing philosophy.

The core CLI and mock provider are free forever. Studio features scale with paid plans when
you need more depth — but your data always stays on your machine.

## Why local-first keeps pricing low

Because InterviewOps runs locally, there are no server costs per session. You bring your
own API keys (BYOK) for AI providers. This means we can offer competitive pricing while
keeping your data private.

---

## Why we don't sell live interview cheating tools

We believe in building real skills. Tools that inject answers during live interviews harm
candidates long-term — they get hired for roles they cannot perform, which leads to
performance issues, stress, and termination.

InterviewOps is built for the candidate who wants to genuinely improve. Our business
model aligns with your long-term success, not a shortcut that could damage your career.

See [ETHICS.md](./ETHICS.md) for our full policy.
