I just open-sourced **InterviewOps Studio** — a local-first AI interview coach for developers.

Most interview AI tools focus on helping you cheat during live interviews.
InterviewOps is the opposite: it's built to help you prepare *before* interviews and build real skill.

**What it does:**

✅ ChatGPT-like mock interview sessions in your browser
✅ Human avatar coach that reacts to your session state
✅ Developer-specific tracks: Angular, React, AI engineer, fullstack, senior frontend, and more
✅ 13 interview modes: behavioral, coding, system design, frontend architecture, AI engineering...
✅ Resume/JD-aware personalized questions
✅ Readiness reports with hire signal, weakness map, and 30-day study plan
✅ STAR story bank for behavioral prep
✅ Progress dashboard
✅ Fully local — your data never leaves your machine
✅ Ethics guardrails built in

**It's open-source. Free to run locally. MIT licensed.**

The mock provider works with zero API keys — no account, no subscription needed to try it.
