# Show HN: InterviewOps Studio — local-first AI interview coach for developers

**Title:** Show HN: InterviewOps Studio – local Angular app for AI interview prep (no live cheating)

**Body:**

InterviewOps Studio is a local-first AI interview practice tool for developers.

It runs entirely on your machine: an Angular UI at localhost:4200 and an Express API at localhost:4317. No account required, no data leaves your machine.

**What it does:**
- ChatGPT-like mock interview sessions with a human avatar coach
- 9 role tracks (Angular, React, AI engineer, senior frontend, etc.)
- 13 interview modes (behavioral, coding, system design, Angular, React, AI-assisted engineering...)
- Resume/JD upload → personalized questions
- Readiness reports: hire signal, weakness map, 30-day study plan
- STAR story bank for behavioral prep
- Progress dashboard
- Export to Markdown/HTML

**What makes it different:**
Most interview AI tools in this space focus on live interview assistance — feeding you answers in real-time during actual interviews. InterviewOps deliberately does not do this. Every session includes an ethics notice and the product is scoped to practice-only.

**Tech stack:** TypeScript, Angular 19 standalone, Express, Vitest, Docusaurus

**Mock provider** works with zero API keys. You can also plug in OpenAI, Anthropic, or Gemini.
