# Ethics Policy

InterviewOps is built on a simple principle: **help developers build real skill, not fake signals**.

This document defines the boundaries of acceptable use.

---

## What InterviewOps Is For

InterviewOps is permitted and encouraged for:

- **Practicing before your interview** — run mock sessions, review questions, rehearse answers
- **Improving communication skills** — structure your answers, refine your storytelling
- **Reviewing technical knowledge** — identify gaps and study the right topics
- **Preparing behavioral stories** — build a library of real experiences in STAR format
- **Understanding weak areas** — use rubric-based feedback to prioritize preparation
- **Practicing AI-assisted engineering discussions** — understand how to talk about ethical AI use
- **Building genuine confidence** — through preparation, not deception

---

## What InterviewOps Is NOT For

InterviewOps explicitly prohibits and does not support:

- **Secret live interview assistance** — using this tool during an active interview session
- **Hidden screen overlays** — running the tool invisibly during a video interview
- **Screen-share evasion** — any technique to hide the tool from interviewers
- **Real-time answer injection** — feeding generated answers into an interview live
- **Impersonation** — using AI-generated content to misrepresent someone else's skills
- **Misrepresenting your skills** — presenting AI-generated knowledge as your own without understanding
- **Violating company interview policies** — many companies explicitly prohibit AI assistance during interviews

---

## Why These Boundaries Exist

**Fairness**: Other candidates prepare honestly. Using live AI assistance is unfair to them.

**Integrity**: Companies hire people to do real work. If you deceive them about your skills, you will struggle in the role — and they will lose trust in you.

**Legal and professional risk**: Some companies have policies that treat live AI assistance as a violation of their interview terms. This can result in offer rescindment, termination, or blacklisting.

**Your growth**: The interview process is designed to reveal genuine capability gaps. If you bypass it with live assistance, you miss the chance to identify and address those gaps. You end up in a role you're not ready for.

---

## Ethics in Every Session

Every session generated by InterviewOps includes an `ethics-notice.md` file that restates these principles. This is intentional. Every practice session is a reminder of the purpose: **genuine preparation, not deception**.

---

## Reporting Misuse

If you discover a fork or modification of InterviewOps that has been repurposed for live interview cheating, please open an issue or contact the maintainer. We take this seriously.

---

## Maintainer Commitment

The maintainers of InterviewOps commit to:

- Never adding live interview assistance features
- Rejecting PRs that introduce cheating workflows
- Maintaining the ethics notice in all session outputs
- Making the ethical use policy clearly visible in all documentation

---

*InterviewOps: practice hard, interview well, build real skill.*
