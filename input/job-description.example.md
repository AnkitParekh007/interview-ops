## About Meridian Labs

Meridian Labs builds developer productivity tools powered by AI. Our products are used by 80,000+ engineers at companies ranging from Series A startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. We're a 120-person company with a strong engineering culture, a distributed team, and a bias for shipping.

---

## Role Overview

We're looking for a **Senior AI Frontend Engineer** to join our Platform Engineering team. You'll own the frontend architecture of our AI-powered code review and developer assistant products, working closely with product, design, and ML engineers to build the next generation of developer tooling.

This is a high-impact role. You'll have significant ownership over how 80,000+ developers interact with AI every day.

---

## What You'll Do

- Architect and build production-grade AI frontend experiences: streaming responses, tool-call rendering, agentic UI patterns, and multi-step workflows
- Lead frontend architecture decisions for new product initiatives — from design review through deployment
- Define and evolve our frontend platform standards: design system, testing patterns, performance monitoring, accessibility
- Partner with ML engineers to understand model capabilities and design UI that communicates AI confidence, uncertainty, and limitations honestly
- Mentor 2-3 mid-level engineers on the team through code reviews, 1:1s, and pair programming
- Contribute to open-source tools we use and build

---

## What We're Looking For

**Required:**
- 5+ years of production frontend engineering experience
- Deep expertise in Angular or React (both is a plus), TypeScript, and modern CSS
- Experience building AI-powered product features: streaming APIs, LLM integrations, tool-call handling
- Strong system design fundamentals: state management, performance optimization, accessibility
- Demonstrated ability to lead projects and make architectural decisions independently
- Experience with design systems and component library development
- Strong communication skills — ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams

**Preferred:**
- Experience with Angular Signals, NgRx, or Zustand at scale
- Contributions to open-source projects
- Experience with AI/ML observability, evaluation, or responsible AI principles
- Conference speaking or technical writing
