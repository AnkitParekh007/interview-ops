# Governance

## Maintainer

InterviewOps is maintained by **Akki Parekh** ([@AnkitParekh007](https://github.com/AnkitParekh007)).

## Decision Making

- **Bug fixes and small improvements**: PRs are reviewed and merged by the maintainer. Contributors are welcome to open PRs without prior discussion.
- **New features**: Open an issue first to discuss the proposal before investing time in a PR. This prevents wasted effort.
- **Breaking changes**: Require maintainer approval and are documented in [CHANGELOG.md](./CHANGELOG.md).
- **Ethics policy changes**: Require maintainer approval. The core principle — no live interview cheating — is non-negotiable.

## Becoming a Maintainer

Active contributors who have made multiple quality contributions may be invited to become maintainers. Maintainers get write access to the repository and can merge PRs.

## Roadmap Decisions

The [ROADMAP.md](./ROADMAP.md) is maintained by the core maintainer. Community input via GitHub Discussions and Issues is welcome and actively considered.

## Forking

This project is MIT licensed. You are free to fork it. If you fork it and add live interview cheating features, please do not call it InterviewOps or imply affiliation with this project.
